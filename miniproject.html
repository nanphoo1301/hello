SCHOOL OVERVIEW

HAKAN Academy (HAKN) was established in January 2015.

The school is currently located at No. 13 ,University Avenue Road, Titan Township, Los Angeles.

The school runs classes in Early Years (Nursery, Reception) and Years 1-13.

A new campus located in Manchester opened in December 2021. This campus is providing classes from Nursery to Year 9 for the 2022-2023 academic year.
